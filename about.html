Who We Are ?

TWOMO is a design firm that provides to the clients buildings and spaces that respond to the aesthetic and cultural values of the surrounding community. We serve our clients with superior designs that are the result of a dialogue with the client that allows us to meet and enhance their needs and vision.

MORE THAN THE TYPICAL PLANNING OR ARCHITECTURAL FIRM.

TWOMO is a versatile team of design and planning professionals whose expertise lies in shaping the natural built environment.

A RECOGNIZED LEADER IN THE DESIGN INDUSTRY.

TWOMO excels in providing Total Vision Design solutions as well as discrete Architecture, Land Planning, and Landscape Architecture services to public and private clients nationwide.

The firm’s integrated, multi-disciplined approach ensures that all TWOMO designs are consistently more contextual, site-sensitive, and richer in overall value, quality, and detail.

We specialize in creating sustainable urban place making, with a focus on housing, retail, office and hospitality in a mixed use format.

Our practice is based on providing creative, flexible and economically sound solutions to strengthen and enliven the built environment.

OUR STORY

TWOMO was established in 2019 to provide comprehensive facilities and environmental consulting services. TWOMO has expanded our service lines to respond to the needs of the marketplace and better serve our clients. With our corporate headquarter in Cairo - Egypt, we provide consulting services to clients throughout the nation.

At TWOMO we are only successful when we help our clients become successful. We believe in doing the job right the first time – on time. In recent surveys, our clients ranked our ability to stay on or ahead of schedule and our technical expertise as the top reasons why they continue to work with TWOMO. Our experienced, diversified team can provide all of the services needed for a successful project and long-lasting client relationship. We can be your single source provider for all of your consulting needs or participate as part of your project team. For the past years and many more to come… our focus is on your success!
