OUR SERVICES

Architecture

FORUM Architecture, offers a full line of architectural services. We will work with our clients from early conceptual design all the way through construction administration. We specialize in residential, commercial, office, retail, mixed-use, religious, hospitality, educational, and civic projects. We work with remodels, additions, “facelifts”/facade remakes, and new construction. We welcome the opportunity to work with our neighbors in the greater Tampa Bay area to clients nationwide. The sooner our clients get us involved on a project, the more holistic the vision will be.

Consulting

  • Assistance in home site/building site selections.
  • Pre-purchase review as to feasibility of site as it pertains to programmatic requirements of project.
  • Marketing brochures.
  • Architectural photography coordination/assistance.
  • Zoning and code analysis assistance with permits and applications.
  • Assistance with contractor selection.
  • Architectural critique of existing designs.

Feasibility
studies

The primary purpose of creating a feasibility study is to simply explore the potential of a given site; thus creating a point of beginning. We work closely with clients to create a series of concepts that will provide a foundation for fundraising, financial decisions, marketing, and community involvement. The feasibility study is the spark that can ignite a dream into fruition.

Master
planning

Master planning takes the approved feasibility study of a larger or more complicated site and develops it into an overall vision. It creates an architectural “vocabulary” to be used throughout the project. Master planning is an integral part of creating a holistic design vision.

Space
planning

Space planning is a tool that can be implemented on many types of projects. On a broader scale it can allow us to create spatial adjacencies and visual axes. On a more pedestrian scale it can allow us to analyze one space at a time in terms of square footage and furniture schemes.

Interiors

Everything matters in terms of design. Every object, color, and texture has the potential to create a new form of beauty. Well thought out interiors can truly take a project to the next level. We specialize in forging intriguing environments.

Concept
Development

Do you have a great idea for a restaurant, store, shop, cafe, or anything in between? If you do you will need to take your concept and develop it into an architectural language in order to brand yourself to the public. Let us take your concept from sketches, through schematic design and design development until your concept is a fully functioning prototype. Once the prototype is just how you want it and your company becomes a franchise, we will become your franchise architecture firm to make sure each and every store, restaurant, shop, etc. maintains the total quality assurance you demand and your patrons have come to love and expect.

Exhibition
Booths Services

Our company includes complete Booth design, organization, and coordination and production department. We offer you several variants of booth designs that meet your company’s image and concept. This design model is then converted into reality so you can accomplish your exhibition goals. Our services include: Project Management, Planning, Logistics, Branding, Site Research, Program Developing, Concept, Detailed Design, 3D and 2D Presentations, Multimedia Development, Animation, 3D Modeling, Construction, Prototyping and Manufacturing.

Furniture, Fixtures
& Equipment

Some spaces call for a custom approach to furniture and object design. We possess the imagination and expertise to create inspiring pieces for any budget. Our approach is to integrate the interior design of our projects into the architecture itself.
